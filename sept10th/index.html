A few days before the final FCC deadline for open Internet comments, we are making an open call for action on September 10th called The Internet Slowdown. This day will be impossible for the FCC, lawmakers, and the world to ignore and we will ensure that we get real Net Neutrality rules. We have sites signed up to go big, we just need you too. If everyone who sees this page spreads the word, we will make it very clear that the Internet, free speech, and freedom of innovation wins for keeps. 

The theme of the action is to make clear that our sites would end up in a slow lane if the ISPs have their way. On September 10th, sites and apps can show images of the spinning wheel of death and send users to battleforthenet.com to file comments and send letters to those who need to hear from us most — the FCC, Congress, and the White House. We will have these assets available for use. Please sign up to get the latest information and help make this a day to remember.

Get the Modal / Banner Widget

Embed our action modal / banner on your site with just one line of Javascript code. This will show visitors a "loading" animation and prompt them to take action. We're working on a new modal experience for our September 10th action, but the banner version is ready! Be sure to sign up for email updates to get the latest information!

Light version:

  var _bftn_options = { animation: 'banner' }
</script>

    async></script>
